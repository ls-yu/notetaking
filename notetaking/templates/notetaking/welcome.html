Note-Taking services ensure that all students have equal access to in-class information. Students with chronic illnesses who must often miss class, students with dyslexia, and more may benefit from note-taking services. Many colleges and universities offer note-taking services through Disability Services. Howevever, note taking services are not as robustly offered in K-12 schools.

NoteServe is a platform to streamline K-12 note-taking services.

Users can register as a Teacher, Note-Taker, or Note-Requester. Teachers create classes and students join through class codes for easy note sharing. Teachers and Note-Takers can post class notes for Note-Requesters to view.
